Privacy Policy

Last updated: September 26, 2025

1. Introduction

Exomiser App ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our genomic variant analysis platform.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Personal Information

  • Account registration information (name, email address, institution)
  • Authentication credentials
  • Profile information and preferences
  • Communication records and support requests

2.2 Genomic Data

  • VCF (Variant Call Format) files
  • Phenotype data and HPO (Human Phenotype Ontology) terms
  • Analysis results and annotations
  • Phenopacket data structures

2.3 Technical Information

  • Usage logs and access patterns
  • System performance metrics
  • Browser information and device characteristics
  • IP addresses and session data

3. How We Use Your Information

  • Provide genomic variant analysis and interpretation services
  • Process and analyze uploaded genomic data
  • Generate phenotype-driven analysis reports
  • Maintain and improve our analysis algorithms
  • Provide technical support and customer service
  • Ensure platform security and prevent unauthorized access
  • Comply with legal and regulatory requirements

4. Data Security and Storage

4.1 Security Measures

  • Industry-standard encryption for data in transit and at rest
  • Access controls and authentication mechanisms
  • Regular security audits and vulnerability assessments
  • Secure Docker containerization and isolated processing environments

4.2 Data Retention

Genomic data and analysis results are retained for the duration necessary to provide our services and as required by applicable laws. Users may request data deletion in accordance with applicable data protection regulations.

5. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal or genomic data to third parties, except in the following circumstances:

  • With your explicit consent
  • To comply with legal obligations or valid legal requests
  • To protect the rights, property, or safety of our users or the public
  • With service providers who assist in platform operations (under strict confidentiality agreements)

6. Your Rights and Choices

  • Access to your personal and genomic data
  • Correction of inaccurate information
  • Deletion of your data (subject to legal and operational constraints)
  • Data portability and export options
  • Opt-out of certain data processing activities
  • Withdrawal of consent where applicable

7. International Data Transfers

Your data may be processed and stored in servers located in different jurisdictions. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place for international data transfers in compliance with applicable data protection laws.

8. Children's Privacy

Our platform is not intended for use by individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 18 years of age.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.

10. Contact Information

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or our privacy practices, please contact us:

Address: Genomic Analysis Team
